Engaging in the Food and Beverage Industry

What is Food and Beverages?

Food and Beverages refers to the industry and products related to the
preparation, distribution, and consumption of food and drinks. It also
includes the service of food and beverages and effects of food and
beverages like food allergens and more.

What is Food?

Food is any substance that an organism consumes for nutritional nourishment.
Food is often derived from plants, animals, or fungi and provides vital nutrients
such as carbs, lipids, proteins, vitamins, and minerals. The material is consumed by an
organism and incorporated by its cells in order to give energy, sustain life, or stimulate growth.
Different animal species have different feeding habits that satisfy their metabolic requirements and
have evolved to fill a specific ecological niche within specific geographical contexts.

What is Beverage?

A beverage, often known as a drink, is a liquid designed for human consumption.
Drinks have major cultural roles in addition to their simple function of quenching thirst.
Drinks that are commonly consumed include ordinary drinking water, milk,
juice, smoothies, and soft drinks. Coffee, tea, and hot chocolate are traditional warm beverages.
There is a lengthy history of caffeinated drinks that include the stimulant caffeine.
